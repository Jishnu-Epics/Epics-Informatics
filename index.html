What is ERPNext?

  • ERPNext, in 100+ languages, is the world’s top 100% free open-source integrated Enterprise Resource Planning Software developed by Frappé Technologies Pvt. Ltd. and is built on MariaDB database system using a Python based server-side Frappe framework.
  • ERPNext is a fully featured Business Management Solution that helps SMEs and MNCs to record all the business transactions in one system by multiple users for multiple processes. ERPNext is trusted by satisfied Users in 150+ Countries. ERPNext is build for various business verticals like Manufacturing, Distribution, Retail, Services, Education, Healthcare, Non-Profit, Agriculture, Hospitality and more…
